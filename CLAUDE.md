# Options Alert System — Claude Instructions

## Working Style

- Do NOT ask permission before making changes. Just make them.
- Do NOT ask "shall I proceed?" or "would you like me to..." — just do it.
- Do NOT summarize what you plan to do before doing it. Act first, summarize after.
- When given a task, execute it immediately, commit, and push.
- Only ask a question if you are genuinely missing information needed to proceed.
- If something is ambiguous, make the best institutional design choice and proceed.
- After completing work, give a brief summary of what changed and any issues found.
